# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

## Our pledge / 我们的承诺

We want this tutorial to stay a welcoming space for anyone learning finance — whether you are a complete beginner, a self-taught retail trader, a student, or a quant veteran looking for a refresher.

我们希望这个教程保持一个对所有学习者都友好的空间 —— 无论你是完全的新手、自学的散户、学生、还是想回顾基础的量化老手。

## Our standards / 行为规范

Things we expect from everyone in this community / 我们期待每个人：

- **Be kind to learners.** Nobody was born knowing what convexity is. Assume the person asking a "simple" question is future-you.
  **对学习者友善。** 没有人生下来就懂凸性。把那个问"简单问题"的人当成未来的自己。
- **Be specific when you disagree.** "This formula is wrong because when σ=0 it divides by zero" is useful. "Bad take" is not.
  **不同意时说得具体。** "这个公式在 σ=0 时会除以零" 是有用的；"垃圾观点" 不是。
- **Respect different learning paces.** People read at different speeds and in different orders. That's fine.
  **尊重不同的学习节奏。** 每个人阅读速度和顺序不一样，没关系。
- **Give credit.** If you learned something from another tutorial, book, or paper — cite it.
  **给出处。** 如果某个思路来自别的教程、书、论文，请注明。

Things we will not tolerate / 我们不接受：

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination of any kind.
  任何形式的人身攻击、骚扰或歧视。
- Financial advice disguised as teaching ("you should buy X tomorrow"). This is a tutorial, not a signal group.
  伪装成教学的投资建议（"你明天应该买 X"）。这是教程，不是喊单群。
- Promoting scams, pump-and-dumps, or predatory platforms.
  推广诈骗、拉高出货、或掠夺性平台。
- Spam, off-topic self-promotion, or doxxing.
  刷屏、与主题无关的自我推广、或人肉搜索。

## Enforcement / 执行

Issues or PRs that violate the above may be edited, closed, or have their authors blocked at the maintainer's discretion. If you see something, feel free to open an issue or email the maintainer privately.

违反以上规范的 issue 或 PR 可能被编辑、关闭，或作者被 ban —— 由维护者裁量。看到不当行为欢迎开 issue 或直接私信维护者。
