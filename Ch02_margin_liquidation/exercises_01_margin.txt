题目 1（保证金计算） / Exercise Set 1 (Margin Calculations)

1) 已知：账户杠杆为100，账户净值为10,000美元，黄金当前卖价为2300.50，买价为2300.70。若客户以2手卖出黄金。
Given: leverage 1:100, account equity USD 10,000, gold bid 2300.50 and ask 2300.70. The client sells 2 lots of gold.
— 黄金的合约量是多少？ / What is the contract size of gold?
ans: 黄金的合约量是 100 盎司/手 / Gold contract size = 100 ounces per lot (100 oz / lot).

— 所需保证金是多少？ / What margin is required?
ans: Margin = Lots × Contract × Price / Leverage = 2 × 100 × 2300.50 / 100 = 4,601.00 USD

— 点值是多少？ / What is the pip value?
ans: Pip value = TickSize × Contract = 0.01 × 100 = 1 USD / point

12) 已知：账户杠杆为500，账户净值为800美元，白银当前价格为26.910。若客户买入1手，爆仓比例为30%。
Given: leverage 1:500, equity USD 800, silver at 26.910. The client buys 1 lot; stop-out ratio = 30 %.
— 所需保证金是多少？ / Required margin?
ans: Margin = Lots × Contract × Price / Leverage = 1 × 5,000 × 26.910 / 500 = 269.10 USD

— 点值是多少？ / Pip value?
ans: Pip value = TickSize × Contract = 0.001 × 5,000 = 5 USD / point

— 爆仓时的价格是多少？ / Liquidation price?
ans: Long → the price must fall to trigger liquidation. Liquidation condition: Equity = Margin × Stop-out Ratio.
     Solve: 800 − (26.910 − LiqPrice) × 1 × 5,000 = 269.10 × 0.30
     → Stop-out price = 26.766 USD.
